Buisness Requirements

To build an MVP which allows our high schoolers to see list of volunteering opportunities in the community.

Ensure that user can select jobs of their interest.

Allow user to create a profile and see the filtered list of jobs.

Allows both parties to establish communication in a safe environment.

The Problem

It's not so easy to find volunteer opportunities.

No way to reach out to people who are in need.

No platform to communicate directly for this kind of mutual aid.

Information available about these jobs is highly limited.

Safety issue for minors

Audience

High Schoolers/ Young Adults

People who are in need of a help in the community (Elderly, physically challenged, parents of young students etc)

Solutions

App allows user to see the list of jobs nearby which are interesting.

User can filter the results on the basis of available days and hours

Background check feature makes it safe for everyone

User can direct communicate with people who seeks help in a open room kind of setup. Not one on one chat.

Accessibility

1. Hierarchy
2. Navigation bar with picture icons
3. Use of familiar format of elements.
4. Back and next click navigation
5. Followed mental model especially in opportunities list
6. Font sizes and Font weights
7. Alternate text
8. Accessible color palette
