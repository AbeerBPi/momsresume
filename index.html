About Me

UI/UX Designer & Web Developer.

My parents say I was born with a paintbrush in my hand. I’ve been painting and sketching for as long as I’ve been walking and talking. I spent the majority of my childhood producing artwork around my family, friends, and cousins, and discussing aspects of this artwork that I can improve on.

By the time I was a high schooler, I was constantly participating in STEM and visual arts competitions, and I don’t mean to brag, but I won pretty much every single one. Now, by this time in my life I wished to pursue the field of Architectural Engineering as the thorough brainstorming and frequent collaboration fascinated my young self. However, due to monetary restrictions, I was unable to attend the prestigious universities I was accepted to, and rather settled for local colleges where I simultaneously received a Masters for the Arts and a Diploma for C++, Java, HTML, and CSS.

I am thankful that I made the decision to pursue these two fields as the duality of my interests enabled me to broaden my perspective on different groups of people and provide me unique insights into characteristics and behaviors of people across disciplines. This allows me to approach certain issues at hand from different and unique angles, always keeping my end user in mind and being conscious of their desires and needs.

Fun Fact

My family and friends nicknamed me Mother Teresa for my delicate personality and natural tendency to be empathetic towards animals, people, and even the Earth. I’ve always felt inclined to relate to others’ issues and step into their shoes, and solve their problems through personalized means of assistance, giving me valuable experience on advocacy of my users utilizing a wide variety of solutions.
